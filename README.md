# Telecom Customer Churn & Revenue Risk Dashboard

## Overview

This Power BI project analyzes telecom customer churn and identifies revenue at risk due to customer attrition.

The dashboard provides executive-level insights into churn behavior across contract types, internet services, and payment methods.

## Tools Used

- Power BI
- Power Query
- DAX
- Excel

## Business Problem

Customer churn directly impacts revenue and profitability.

This dashboard helps stakeholders:

- Measure churn rate
- Identify high-risk customer segments
- Quantify revenue at risk
- Understand churn drivers

## Key Metrics

| KPI | Value |
|------|--------|
| Total Customers | 7,043 |
| Churned Customers | 1,869 |
| Churn Rate | 26.5% |
| Revenue At Risk | $139K |

## Key Insights

- Month-to-Month customers account for the majority of churn.
- Fiber Optic customers show significantly higher churn.
- Electronic Check customers represent the highest-risk payment segment.
- Contract type and internet service are the strongest churn indicators.
